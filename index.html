What we have to offer:

We offer a Bed and Breakfast,a mini Pet Zoo and great hospitality. The cabin is furnished with bed , couch table, mini fridge, TV and internet. The bed is located up in the loft. There is also small fire pit to the left of the cabin that is available to use as well.
